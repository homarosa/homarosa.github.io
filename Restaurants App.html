Chefoo is a B2B app that helps restaurant staff manage orders,
update menus, and review sales statements efficiently.

My Role

This is the first mobile app I designed, based on a set of initial requirements. As a UX/UI Designer, my responsibilities included:

  • • Analyze requirements and establishing the Information Architecture.
  • • Design user flows and interactions.
  • • Deliver high-fidelity visuals and UI assets.

Tools

User Research

  • Primary users:Staff at partner restaurants (using the app for order management).
  • Secondary Users: Marketing and sales teams (from the food delivery platform).

  • Restaurant Side Requirements:

  • • Order Management:
  • ◦ Chefs need a full list of orders;
  • ◦ A list of dishes sorted by order for packaging labeling;
  • ◦ A list of dishes sorted by region to assist drivers with distribution.
  • • Driver Coordination:
  • ◦ Access to driver's contact details;
  • ◦ Ability to scan the driver's QR code for order handoff.
  • • Financial Tracking:
  • ◦ Access to monthly sales statements.

  • Delivery Platform Side Requirements:

  • • Menu Management:
  • ◦ Restaurant staff can update their menus directly in the app, to reduce customer service requests;
  • • VIP Benefits:
  • ◦ Apply discounts for VIP users dining in partner restaurants;
  • • Sales Insights:
  • ◦ Provide in-app sales data analytics (via in-app purchase).

Ideation

The core features were developed through collaborative interviews with:

  • ◦ The UX designer of the consumer-facing app;
  • ◦ The lead developer of the driver delivery platform;
